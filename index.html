Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how this application ("the App") handles user data.

Information Collection and Use

The App does not require user registration and does not directly collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or phone number.

The App allows users to submit food-related data (such as images or text descriptions) in order to calculate calorie information.

Data Processing

To provide its functionality, the App sends user-provided data (such as images or descriptions of food) to third-party AI services for processing.

These services may include providers such as Anthropic (Claude) and Google (Gemini). Data is processed according to their respective privacy policies.

Data Storage

The App does not store personal data on servers controlled by the developer.

Data may be processed temporarily by third-party services to generate results.

Data Sharing

The App does not sell or share user data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Security

Data is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

User Rights

Since the App does not store personal data, users are not required to request data deletion. Users can control their data by managing content on their own device.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
