Delete your Langsy account

App: Langsy (developer: KitchenTech) · Last updated: April 21, 2026

This page explains how to delete your Langsy account and all associated data.

Option 1 — Delete inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open Langsy on your iPhone or Android device.
  2. Go to the Profile tab (bottom-right).
  3. Scroll down and tap "Privacy & Data" (Приватность и данные).
  4. At the bottom, under "Danger Zone" (Опасная зона), tap "Delete account" (Удалить аккаунт).
  5. Confirm the deletion in the dialog. Your account and data will be removed immediately and permanently.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app (e.g., lost device, locked out), send an email to sergmochalin@gmail.com with:

  • Subject: Langsy Account Deletion Request
  • Your registered email address (the one tied to your Google or Apple Sign-In)
  • Optional: reason for deletion (helps us improve — not required)

We will process the request within 7 business days and send you confirmation once completed.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Account record (email, display name, profile picture URL, linked Google/Apple identifier)
  • All chat / conversation history with the AI tutor
  • Language level (CEFR) and progress history
  • Saved idioms
  • Listening practice sessions and results
  • Speech repeat-practice sessions and scores
  • Daily usage counters
  • Uploaded avatar image (if any)

What is retained (and for how long)

We keep the following anonymized data, with no link back to you, for legitimate operational purposes:

  • AI usage accounting logs (provider, model, token counts, cost) — up to 90 days, solely for cost tracking and abuse prevention. Your user_id is set to NULL upon account deletion, making these records non-personal.
