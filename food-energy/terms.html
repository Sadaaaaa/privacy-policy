1. Description of Service

Food Energy is a mobile application that uses artificial intelligence to estimate the nutritional content of food from photos and text descriptions, and to look up packaged products by barcode. The App stores your meal history and preferences locally on your device. AI requests are routed through a thin proxy server we operate (Cloudflare Worker) and forwarded to third-party AI providers; barcode lookups are sent to Open Food Facts.

Nutritional estimates are approximate and should not be used as a substitute for professional dietary advice.
