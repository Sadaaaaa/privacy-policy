Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Food Energy ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Data stored on your device

All meal records, calorie data, macro nutrients, and food photos are stored locally on your device using an on-device database (SQLite). This data never leaves your device except as described below.

1.2 Data sent to AI services

When you scan food or add a meal manually, the App routes your photo or text description through a thin proxy server we operate (Cloudflare Worker) and forwards it to one of three third-party AI services for nutritional analysis:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — primary analysis provider
  • Google (Gemini API) — fallback provider
  • Mistral AI — fallback provider

The proxy does not store your photos or descriptions — it forwards them in real time and may briefly log only request metadata (IP address, timestamp) for short-term abuse prevention, retained no longer than 7 days. We do not link this metadata to your identity. Images and text are sent solely for the purpose of food recognition and nutritional estimation. No personally identifiable information is attached to these requests.

1.3 Barcode lookups

When you scan a packaged product's barcode, the App sends only the decoded barcode number to Open Food Facts, an open community database, to retrieve nutritional information and a product image. No personal data is shared.

1.4 Subscription data

If you subscribe, payment and subscription management are handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We use RevenueCat to verify subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID — no personal information is shared.

1.5 Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or phone number
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising
  • We do not store your photos or meal descriptions on our servers

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Food photos and text: sent to AI providers exclusively for nutritional analysis, then discarded by the provider per their data retention policies
  • Barcodes: sent to Open Food Facts to retrieve product nutrition
  • Meal history: stored locally for your personal tracking
  • Subscription status: checked via RevenueCat to unlock premium features
  • Proxy request metadata: short-term IP and timestamp logs used only for abuse prevention

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Data is only transmitted to the service providers listed above for the specific purposes described.

4. Data Retention

All meal data is stored on your device only. Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all stored data. AI providers process images and text in real-time and do not retain them beyond their standard API processing windows. Proxy abuse-prevention logs, if any, are retained no longer than 7 days.

5. Data Security

All communication between the App, our proxy, and AI providers is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. On-device data is protected by your device's built-in security (passcode, biometrics, encryption).

6. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from any user, regardless of age. If you believe we have inadvertently received such information from a child, please contact us at sergmochalin@gmail.com so we can address it.

7. Your Rights

Since all meal data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can:

  • View all your data within the App (History screen)
  • Delete individual meals or all data from the App settings
  • Uninstall the App to permanently remove all locally stored data

8. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services. Please refer to their privacy policies:

  • Anthropic (Claude) Privacy Policy
  • Google (Gemini) Privacy Policy
  • Mistral AI Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • Open Food Facts Terms of Use
  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at sergmochalin@gmail.com.
