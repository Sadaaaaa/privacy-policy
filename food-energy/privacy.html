Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Food Energy ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Data stored on your device

All meal records, calorie data, macro nutrients, and food photos are stored locally on your device using an on-device database (SQLite). This data never leaves your device except as described below.

1.2 Data sent to AI services

When you scan food, the App sends your food photo or text description to third-party AI services for nutritional analysis:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — primary analysis provider
  • Google (Gemini API) — fallback provider
  • Mistral AI — fallback provider

Images and text are sent solely for the purpose of food recognition and nutritional estimation. We do not send any personally identifiable information along with these requests.

1.3 Subscription data

If you subscribe, payment and subscription management are handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We use RevenueCat to verify subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID — no personal information is shared.

1.4 Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or phone number
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising
  • We do not store your photos on any server

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Food photos and text: sent to AI providers exclusively for nutritional analysis, then discarded by the provider per their data retention policies
  • Meal history: stored locally for your personal tracking
  • Subscription status: checked via RevenueCat to unlock premium features

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Data is only transmitted to the AI service providers listed above for the sole purpose of food analysis.

4. Data Retention

All meal data is stored on your device only. Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all stored data. AI providers process images and text in real-time and do not retain them beyond their standard API processing windows.

5. Data Security

Communication with AI services is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. On-device data is protected by your device's built-in security (passcode, biometrics, encryption).

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can:

  • View all your data within the App (History screen)
  • Delete individual meals or all data from the App settings
  • Uninstall the App to permanently remove all data

8. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services. Please refer to their privacy policies:

  • Anthropic (Claude) Privacy Policy
  • Google (Gemini) Privacy Policy
  • Mistral AI Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact
